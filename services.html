Church Services & Youth Organization

At Gospel Celebration Centre Ministry (G.C.C.M), we are committed to nurturing the spiritual growth of all age groups, especially our youth. Our church services are designed to inspire, teach, and build a strong sense of community through worship, Bible study, and fellowship.

Youths are an integral part of our congregation, actively participating in church activities and organizing various programs to support the church's mission.

Youth Group Organization

The youth ministry at G.C.C.M is open to all young members of the church. We focus on spiritual growth, community engagement, and leadership development. Below are details on how to join and the activities we undertake:

How to Join the Youth Group

If you're between the ages of 15 and 35, you're welcome to join our youth group. Simply attend one of our youth meetings or speak to one of the youth leaders after Sunday service. New members are always warmly welcomed!

Youth Meetings

  • Youth Bible Study: Every Wednesday at 5 PM
  • Youth Prayer Meeting: Every Saturday at 10 AM
  • Youth Fellowship: Every second Sunday of the month after service
