Church Constitution

We, the members of Gospel Celebration Centre Ministry (G.C.C.M), in the firm belief that Jesus Christ is the head of the Church, do hereby establish this Constitution to guide the governance of the Church and its mission in serving God and the community.

Article I: Name and Purpose

This church shall be known as Gospel Celebration Centre Ministry. The primary purpose of this Church is to glorify God through worship, teaching, discipleship, and community outreach.

Article II: Statement of Faith

  • The inspiration of the Bible, both Old and New Testaments.
  • The deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His atoning death, and His resurrection.
  • Salvation through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.

Article III: Membership

Membership in this Church shall be open to any person who confesses faith in Jesus Christ, has been baptized, and agrees to adhere to the beliefs and practices of the Church.
